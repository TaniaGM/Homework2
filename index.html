Tania

Introduction

I was born and raised in Canada. I am a member of the Gitxsan and Tsimshian Nations located in Northern British Columbia. My Gitxsan name is Lu'al'alyax. Translated into English it means, "Talking from the heart/Talking from within." My brothers say it means, "Talks too much/Can't shut up."

What are your hobbies?

My hobbies include, but are not limited to, traveling, going to movies, trying to outwit our 2 rescue cats who are constantly trying to kill us.

What's your dream job?

I have always dreamed of being the Head Coach of the Edmonton Oilers, but restraining orders prevent me from realizing my dream.
